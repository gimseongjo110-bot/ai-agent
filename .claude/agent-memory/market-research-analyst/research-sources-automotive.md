Highly reliable sources for automotive market research:

- **IEA Global EV Outlook** (iea.org) — authoritative annual report on global EV sales by region, market share, and forecasts. Published May each year. Best source for EV penetration rates and country-level data.
- **Mordor Intelligence** — broad automotive market sizing (TAM); 2025 global auto market: $2.75T, CAGR 3.46% through 2030.
- **Precedence Research** — autonomous vehicle and EV market sizing with detailed CAGR data.
- **Fortune Business Insights** — hybrid vehicle and connected car market sizing.
- **MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal** (marklines.com) — monthly OEM sales data by country, including Korea. Very reliable for unit sales.
- **S&P Global Automotive Insights** — macro outlook, tariff impacts, production forecasts.
- **Hyundai Motor Group newsroom** (hyundaimotorgroup.com) — official Hyundai/Kia annual sales and revenue results.
- **Focus2Move** (focus2move.com) — global OEM ranking and country-level market data.
- **Benchmark Minerals** — EV sales volume tracking.
- **KOTRA** — excellent for Korean market entry and export data.

**Industry terminology:**
- TAM = Total Addressable Market (전체 시장 규모)
- SAM = Serviceable Addressable Market (세분화된 시장)
- CAGR = Compound Annual Growth Rate (연평균 성장률)
- ICE = Internal Combustion Engine (내연기관)
- BEV = Battery Electric Vehicle (순수 전기차)
- HEV = Hybrid Electric Vehicle (하이브리드)
- PHEV = Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (플러그인 하이브리드)
- ADAS = Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (첨단 운전자 보조 시스템)
